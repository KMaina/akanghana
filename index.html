The Akan Naming

The Akan people of Ghana name their children after the day of the week they were born.

Akan is the language of the people called Akans. The Akans are the largest ethnic group in Ghana. According to the 2000, national population census the Ghanaian population is Akans.

These "day names" have further deeper meaning that connects individuals to their heart and soul this is because Akan names are not arbitrary but they are based on socio-cultural and contexts
